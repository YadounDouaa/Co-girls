About Co Girls

Co Girls


Co-Girls is a computer science club dedicated to all girls in IT. Officially launched on November 28, 2019.
Our goal is to create an IT community of girls

Activities

Our Activities

  • CITI Oran'19
  • Public Speaking Work Shop
  • Khotwa
  • Team Management

I. CITI Oran'19

- Co-Girls participated in CITI Oran'19 international conference, where we won the best price in the axis of "IT project in the context of tourism", Our project has been classified as the best project.

II. Public Speaking Workshop

The Co Girls learned how to be a good public speaker, under the supervision and direction of Miss Bengueddach through this workshop. We knew so many things, such as how to present ourselves professionally, how to attract the audience, how to be confident and how to hide the stress from the jurors.

III. Khotwa

The Co-Girls Club was honoured to be present at the #khotwa Student
We received many students from different specialties, they discovered the club and what our signs and passions are, and we shared with them all the activities we are interested in, projects, workshops and many surprises and opportunities.

IV. Team Management

The quarantine seems to bring out the curiosity to learn new things at the Co-Girls as if we were all gathered at the same table and it was possible today thanks to the technology of today and thanks to Mrs. Asmaa Bengueddach and Mr. Fatah Kezzim for animating an online training course that talked about how to manage a team in the presence of other students and teachers from different regions in Algeria. We learned to know the essential tools that characterize a good leader, how to know his leadership profile, methods to work with to achieve a project while having a sense of teamwork. We would like to thank everyone who took part in this training and it will be a start compared to similar trainings especially at this time when we ask everyone to stay strong and look for what concerns you and interests you as favorable hobbies, hidden talents ... The most important thing is that you stay always optimistic and everything will go inshallah.
